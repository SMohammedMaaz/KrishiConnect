Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have

To register as a farmer, click on "Join as Farmer" on the homepage. You'll need to provide your phone number for verification, then complete your profile by entering personal details and Aadhar number for verification. Once verified, you can start listing your products.

KrishiConnect charges a minimal service fee of 2% on successful transactions. This fee helps us maintain the platform, provide customer support, and continue developing new features. There are no registration fees or monthly subscriptions required.

KrishiConnect offers secure payment options including UPI, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. Funds are held in escrow until the buyer confirms receipt of the products. This ensures both parties are protected during transactions. You can withdraw your earnings to your bank account at any time.

To get personalized crop suggestions, login to your farmer dashboard and navigate to the "Crop Advisory" section. Our AI system uses your location, soil type, climate data, and market trends to recommend the best crops to grow. You can also input specific parameters about your farm for more tailored suggestions.
