Our Vision & Mission

We're building a sustainable and efficient agricultural ecosystem

Our Vision

To create a self-reliant farming community with direct access to markets, fair prices, and innovative technologies.

Our Mission

To eliminate middlemen, provide transparent pricing, and leverage technology to empower farmers and connect them directly with buyers.

Our Values

Transparency, Integrity, Innovation, Sustainability, and Community-first approach are at the core of everything we do.

Our Journey

The story of KrishiConnect from inception to impact

2020

The idea of KrishiConnect was born during a rural field study, where we witnessed the challenges faced by farmers in getting fair prices for their produce.

2021

After months of research and planning, we launched the first prototype of KrishiConnect in three districts, connecting 100 farmers with local buyers.

2022

Secured our first round of funding and expanded to multiple states. Introduced AI-powered crop suggestions and weather advisory features.

2023

Reached the milestone of 1000+ farmers and 500+ buyers on the platform. Launched mobile application with multilingual support.

2024

Implemented direct UPI payments, expanded to all major agricultural states, and introduced bulk procurement features for institutional buyers.

2025

Currently serving 5000+ farmers and 2000+ buyers, with continuous innovation and feature enhancements to better serve our growing community.
